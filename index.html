Our Projects

Discover the range of initiatives we're proud to present.
Join us on this journey as we embrace the transformative power of chess and create meaningful connections within the chess community.

Chess in Schools Program

Our Chess in Schools Program aims to bring the educational benefits of chess to students across the region. By partnering with schools, we integrate chess into their curricula, establishing chess clubs and organizing tournaments.


Community Chess Events

Our Community Chess Events are designed to bring together chess enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. These events include chess festivals, simultaneous exhibitions, and interactive workshops.


Chess Outreach Programs

Our Chess Outreach Programs focus on making chess accessible to underprivileged and underserved communities. Through these initiatives, we organize chess workshops, provide chess resources, and offer scholarships for chess training.


We are here for you

Have a question, need assistance, or ready to take the next chess move? Contact us today and let our team at Michael Chess provide you with the support and guidance you need. We're here to help you on your chess journey, so reach out to us and let's make your chess experience extraordinary.

FAQ

+

How do I get started with chess training?

To get started with chess training, you can reach out to us through our contact page or sign up for our training programs directly on our website. Our team of experienced coaches will guide you through the process and help you determine the best training option based on your skill level and goals.

+

Are the chess training programs suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our chess training programs are designed to cater to players of all skill levels, including beginners. Whether you're new to the game or looking to improve your foundational knowledge, our coaches will provide personalized instruction to help you develop your skills and understanding of chess.

+

Can you provide chess training for groups or schools?

Yes, we offer chess training for groups and schools. We have specialized programs designed for schools and educational institutions to integrate chess into their curricula. Additionally, we can arrange group training sessions to cater to multiple participants. Please reach out to us with your specific requirements, and we'll be happy to discuss the options available.

+

Do you organize chess tournaments and events?

Yes, we organize chess tournaments and events regularly. We offer a variety of competitive opportunities for chess enthusiasts of all levels, from local tournaments to open competitions. Stay updated on our website or subscribe to our newsletter to receive information about upcoming tournaments and events.

+

How can I contribute to your chess charity events?

We greatly appreciate your interest in contributing to our chess charity events. You can support our initiatives by participating in the charity tournaments, making donations, or volunteering your time and skills. Please contact us through our dedicated charity events page or get in touch with our team to discuss the specific ways you can contribute.
