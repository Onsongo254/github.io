About US

Welcome to Michael Chess, a platform established in January 2021 by Danstan Moturi. Our journey began as a dedication to my dad, Michael Asati, a true chess enthusiast who embodied great enthusiasm, passion, sacrifice, and generosity for the game.
Inspired by his legacy, we have created Michael Chess to honor his love for chess and continue his mission of promoting and advancing the game.

Our Story

Michael Chess is a tribute to Michael Asati's enduring dedication to chess. As a renowned player, coach, and mentor, he continues to make a significant impact on the chess community in Kenya and East Africa. We honor his legacy by celebrating the beauty and intellectual growth that chess offers.
As the pioneer of the Kisii and Nyamira Chess Clubs, Michael has nurtured young talents and advocated for chess in schools. His leadership within Chess Kenya ensures the growth and success of chess communities nationwide. Together with Michael Asati, we strive to promote and advance the game he loves.

Mission

At Michael Chess, our mission is to provide quality chess coaching, training, and mentorship programs that cater to different skill levels in a supportive and inclusive environment. We are dedicated to offering a range of services, including online and in-person coaching, tournaments, events, and community outreach programs.
Through these initiatives, we aim to promote the benefits of chess for personal development, social interaction, and community engagement.

Vission

Our vision is to create a vibrant and inclusive chess community that inspires and empowers individuals of all ages and backgrounds to discover and develop their passion for chess.
We strive to foster a spirit of teamwork and sportsmanship, where every member feels welcome, respected, and encouraged to participate. By providing a nurturing environment, we aim to cultivate a lifelong love for chess and build lasting connections within the chess community.

Commitments

While pursuing our mission and vision, we face various challenges. The lack of popularity of chess in certain regions, the need to raise the priority of chess in educational and recreational settings, limited financial resources, and the lack of publicity are among the obstacles we aim to overcome.
However, we are committed to addressing these challenges by advocating for chess, seeking partnerships, and leveraging communication channels to raise awareness and engagement.

Our Values

At Michael Chess, we uphold the following values that guide our actions and decisions:

  1. Excellence
  2. Community
  3. Education
  4. Innovation
  5. Fair Play
  6. Respect
  7. Integrity
